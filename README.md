### 👋🏼 Hello there, anon, and welcome to my GitHub!

#### I am a [theoretical physicist by training](https://www.astro.sunysb.edu/steinkirch/)  (B.Sc. at University of Sao Paulo; M.Sc. *en route*, Ph.D. at Stony Brook University; graduate research at the Los Alamos National Laboratory and Brokhaven National Laboratory; undergraduate research at NASA Goddard Space Center) and a [principal software engineer by trade](https://marina.nullstar.fun/) (Shopify, Apple, Etsy, Yelp, startups, and private projects — both as an individual contributor and in leadership roles).


#### I started [coding in middle school](https://web.archive.org/web/20070322015644/http://fly.to/bytegirl) and have spent my entire life working on engineering && science. My story with AI/ML started back in high school when I received the first place on a national competition with Expert Systems ("AI Doctor"), during my PhD when I was working on my thesis on the equation of state of neutron stars at Los Alamos (I also took graduate classes in ML and robotics at that time), then at many moments during my engineering jobs, and now, with LLMs, I have been full-time building, researching, running my fleet of agents, and leveling up my skills in this extraordinary period of technological acceleration.

#### Today, I am building [NULLSTAR GAMES](https://nullstar.games). Stay tuned!
